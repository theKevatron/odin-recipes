Fight Milk

"Fight Milk": For when you need a burst of energy to help you fight like the crow

An energizing drink found in the Charlie Kelly cuisine

Known for putting the body in fight or flight mode, great for a getting you moving and releasing toxins

Ingredients

  • Milk(1 Gallon)
  • Crow's Eggs(Preferably 1 egg per cup, but crow's eggs are hard to come by)
  • Alcohol of Choice

Recipe instructions

  1. Pour milk into a mixing container(a 5 gallon bucket works fine)
  2. Add crow's eggs(Optional: Grind down shells and add them)
  3. Add alcohol of choice
  4. Enjoy the blend that allows the body to more easily absorb the unique source of crowtein
