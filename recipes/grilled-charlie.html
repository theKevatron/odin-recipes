Grilled Charlie

"Grilled Charlie": When made in the correct order, a perfect mix of sweet and salty

A great afternoon snack, found in the Charlie Kelly cuisine

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Cheese of Choice
  • Peanut Butter

Recipe instructions

  1. Place cheese on pan and melt cheese at medium heat
  2. Butter one side of a slice of bread and place it face up(unbuttered side onto the cheese)
  3. Place chocolate on buttered side, then place another slide of bread to trap heat
  4. Place peanut butter on the top of the second slice
  5. Remove from pan once chocolate has softened
